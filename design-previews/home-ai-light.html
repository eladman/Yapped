how it works

From export to leaderboard in about three minutes.

Four steps. No app to install, no account to hand over your chat to.

01

Export the chat

In WhatsApp: chat → more → export chat → without media. You do the export — we never touch WhatsApp.

02

Parse on-device

The file is read and analyzed right in your browser. The raw messages never leave your device.

03

AI writes the quiz

The model turns your real stats and inside jokes into ten questions, with a tone that fits — partner, friends, or family.

04

Send the link

Everyone plays the same quiz free. The leaderboard settles who was actually paying attention.

under the hood

An AI that actually read the chat.

Yapped doesn’t ship your conversation to a server. It extracts signals on your device — who talks most, the running jokes, the peak-chaos hours — and hands only those to the model.

Signals, not transcripts

Stats and a few short quotes leave your browser — never the full chat.

Tone-aware generation

Roast dial scales with the relationship — savage for friends, gentle for family.

English & Hebrew

Questions come back in the language your chat actually speaks.

privacy by design

It never leaves your device.

Privacy isn’t buried in a policy here — it’s the architecture. Here’s exactly where the line sits.

your browser

Stays with you

  • Reads and parses the export file
  • Extracts stats and inside jokes
  • Holds your raw messages, then forgets them
our servers

Never sees

  • Your raw conversation
  • The full message history
  • Anything after the quiz is written
